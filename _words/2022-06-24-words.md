## notes
- Ancient Greek *σχῐ́ζω* (skhízō, "I split")
- cf. PIE *\*skey-* (to split) (whence English *skid*)
- cf. *schizophrenia* <- *σχῐ́ζω* + *φρήν* (phrḗn, "midriff, heart, mind") (cf. *frenetic*)
